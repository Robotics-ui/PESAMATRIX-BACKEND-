2. Cloud-to-Cloud Copy Trading Architecture

Implement pure cloud-to-cloud architecture using MetaApi and CopyFactory.

Do NOT use:

- VPS
- MT4/MT5 terminals on Replit
- Trade copier EAs
- Signal bridges
- Local terminal synchronization

Requirements:

- Allow registration of subscriber/slave MT5 accounts through MetaApi.
- Store MetaApi account IDs.
- Support multiple master accounts.
- Support multiple signal groups.
- Support multiple queues and workers.
- Scale using BullMQ and Redis workers.

3. Trade Replication

Any action on a master account must automatically replicate to active subscribers:

Open Trade:

- Symbol
- Volume
- Buy/Sell

Modify Trade:

- Stop Loss
- Take Profit
- Partial closes
- Volume modifications

Close Trade:

- Full closure

Requirements:

- Use CopyFactory APIs.
- Maintain mapping between master trades and subscriber trades.
- Handle retries and failures through BullMQ.
- Log all replication events.

4. Subscription Enforcement

Only paid subscribers should receive copied trades.

Requirements:

- Before replication validate:
  - Subscription active
  - Payment confirmed
  - Expiry date not reached

If subscription expired:

- Disable copying immediately
- Remove from active replication groups
- Record enforcement action
